Four years ago, he discovered house music and fell in love with it. Two years later, after long lasting listens, he decided to start production and mixing. Six months later, he was elected first producer of the month in the history of the “Chineurs de House” (and released his track Swish on FREECDH001. He did not stop there and started the label “In Any Case Records” with his friends G2S and Johanna, combining Hip Hop, sampled House, Deep House and raw Disco edits. The whizz kid is not only a label founder and producer, he is also a talented DJ who has shared the stage with big names such as Earl Jeffers aka Chesus, Dan Shake, Jus Ed, Linkwood, DJ Gregory. Malouane sees his sets as the encounter of “his influences and the audience expectations”, even when it means taking risks. He constantly aims to get dancefloors to a communion where only looks, dance, music and sharing matter. Able to navigate between styles with ease, he adapts to is audience to create surprising magical moments where time stands still on the dance floor.
