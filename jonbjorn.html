For over a decade now, Jónbjörn has been a regular suspect on the bills of events not only in Reykjavík but also in cities all over Europe such as Berlin, Munich, Helsinki, and Tbilisi, to name a few of his personal favs. Together with Viktor Birgiss, Jónbjörn has cultivated Lagaffe Tales, a record label that has flourished from a passion project to a prominent fixture in Iceland's music scene. Lagaffe Tales serves as a platform for both local talents and international artists, contributing to the label's diverse and dynamic roster. With a relentless drive to push boundaries and explore new artistic territories, Jónbjörn has become a noteworthy figure in the Icelandic music scene and beyond. His unwavering dedication, coupled with Lagaffe Tales commitment to showcasing talent, has contributed to the label's growth and the broader music community in Iceland. As Jónbjörn continues to evolve as a musician and visual artist, his impact on the electronic music landscape is poised to endure.
